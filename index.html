Our Story

A sanctuary above
the clouds

Perched on the Singalila Ridge at 10,000 feet, GreenHill Paradise is a family-run mountain homestay where the boundary between earth and sky genuinely dissolves. Mornings begin with Kanchenjunga glowing in first light; evenings end watching seas of cloud roll through the valleys below.

We are rooted in Nepali and Lepcha culture — offering home-cooked meals, warm beds, prayer flag-strung views, and a connection to the Himalayas that no resort can manufacture.

Plan Your Visit

Every season, a different magic

March – May

Spring

Rhododendrons burst crimson along the ridge. Crystal skies reveal Kanchenjunga in full glory. Perfect trekking weather, gentle warmth.

Best for Trekking
June – September

Monsoon

Dramatic mist pours through valleys. Vivid emerald landscapes. A moody, cinematic atmosphere for the adventurous traveller.

Dramatic Landscapes
October – November

Autumn

Post-monsoon skies are crystal-clear. Golden light on the peaks. Arguably the most photogenic season with sweeping 360° views.

Peak Photography
December – February

Winter

Snow blankets the ridge, prayer flags vivid against white. Cosy fireside evenings and the rare magic of deep Himalayan snowfall.

Snow & Silence

Dining

Taste of the Mountains

Home-cooked Nepali, Tibetan, and Indian meals crafted from fresh local produce and recipes passed down through generations.
